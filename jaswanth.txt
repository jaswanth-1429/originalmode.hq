## Core Requirements (Static)

### Design System
- **Color Palette**: Network-inspired design system
  - Backgrounds: #FAFFEE (page), #FAFAFF (cards), #EDEDFE (subtle), #CACAFC (sections)
  - Text: #004534 (primary), #0C6951 (secondary), #807979 (light)
  - Brand Colors: #D3FF62 (primary), #004534 (dark), #0C6951 (hover)
- **Typography**: Inter font family with responsive sizing
- **UI Components**: Pill-shaped buttons (25-33px border-radius), network cards with hover effects
- **90/10 Color Rule**: Color only on small interactive elements, rest white/light backgrounds

### Page Structure
1. Hero Section - Brand introduction with logo
2. About Section - Philosophy overview
3. Philosophy Section - 4 core pillars (Discipline, Growth, Focus, Power)
4. Products Section - Ecommerce-ready product showcase with filtering
5. Videos Section - YouTube video embeds
6. Quotes Section - Motivational warrior quotes
7. Blog Section - Articles and insights
8. Newsletter Section - Email signup
9. Contact Section - Contact form (mailto functionality)
10. Footer - Comprehensive links and social media

### Features
- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Shopping cart with localStorage persistence

- Product filtering by category
- Smooth scroll navigation
